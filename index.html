WELCOME TO MY SHOP

Art is inseperable from the human condition. Clothing is art. The way we chose to represent ourselves to the world each day is an artistic choice and as such should be thought of with the same depth. Art is always situated within a broader world, a political one, so when climate change and huge corporations are trying to seal the fate of the world with horrendous practices, it is up to us to fight it through sustainable practice, no matter how inconsequential they seem. And despite it all, art should still be fun!
